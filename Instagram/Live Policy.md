Report a live broadcast on Instagram
====================================

Learn how to [report non-live content](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.instagram.com%2F372161259539444) on Instagram.

Reporting a live broadcast from the Instagram app or from a computer on Instagram.com
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you see someone's live broadcast and think that it goes against Instagram's [Community Standards](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftransparency.meta.com%2Fpolicies%2Fcommunity-standards), you can report it. From the live broadcast that you want to report:

Instagram app for iPhone and iPad

1.  Tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=instagram-threedots-shared&density=1) **Options** in the top right of the broadcast.
2.  Tap **Report**, then follow the on-screen instructions.

Instagram app for Android

1.  Tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=ig-three-dots-more-vertical&density=1) **Options** in the top right of the broadcast.
2.  Tap **Report...**, then follow the on-screen instructions.

Instagram.com from a computer

What kinds of live broadcasts can you report on Instagram
---------------------------------------------------------

We recommend reporting any live broadcast that you think goes against our [Community Standards](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftransparency.meta.com%2Fpolicies%2Fcommunity-standards). This can include:

*   Self-harm
*   Suicide
*   Physical abuse
*   Bullying or harassment
*   Child abuse and endangerment
*   Threats of real-world violence

What if you're not sure about reporting a live broadcast on Instagram
---------------------------------------------------------------------

We recommend submitting a report if you think that a live broadcast goes against our Community Standards, but aren't sure if it's being shared to speak out against something or educate others.

If you aren't sure whether a live broadcast is fake or meant to be a joke, we also recommend submitting a report if you think that it goes against our Community Guidelines. The safety of our viewers and creators is our highest priority and your account won't be affected if you submit a report.

What happens when you report a live broadcast on Instagram
----------------------------------------------------------

When you report a live broadcast, Instagram will review it as quickly as possible and remove broadcasts that don't follow our policies. We recognise the urgency of responding to time-sensitive reports while content is streaming live.

Once you've submitted a report, you'll receive a message when your report has been reviewed by Instagram. Creators who've previously posted something that's been removed for going against our Community Standards may not be able to start another live broadcast on Instagram.

When you report a live broadcast, bear in mind that:

*   Your report is anonymous, except if you're [reporting an intellectual property infringement](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.instagram.com%2F126382350847838%3Fhelpref%3Dfaq_content). The broadcast creator won't see who reported them.
*   You may not be able to see what happens to the account that you reported.
*   We may determine that the live broadcast doesn't go against our Community Standards.
*   Depending on the severity of the situation, we may decide to end the live broadcast, disable the account or contact law enforcement.

What if there's another live broadcast issue that you want to report
--------------------------------------------------------------------

*   If you believe that someone is using your copyrighted work without your permission, you can fill in this form to [report an infringement of your rights](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.instagram.com%2Fcontact%2F372592039493026%3Fhelpref%3Dfaq_content). Learn more about [how to report copyright infringement](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.instagram.com%2F277982542336146%3Fhelpref%3Dfaq_content).
*   If you're having trouble viewing a live broadcast, you can report it. Bear in mind that if you're having a problem viewing a live broadcast, it could be because of a network or Internet connection issue.
